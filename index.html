About Us

Women4Life is the first of its kind botique style medical practice - founded in 2012 by Dr Adi-Mari Schoeman and Dr Sita Pretorius.

Our practice has been designed from the ground up to celebrate the uniqueness of women and their specific health needs.  We offer longer appointments, more comprehensive screenings, personalised attention and a feminine touch.

We place a high emphasis on preventitive medical care, aiming to empower and equip each person to live a healthy full life.
