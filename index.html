About us

Women4Life is a boutique style medical practice that was founded in 2012 by Dr Adi-Mari Schoeman and Dr Sita Pretorius.  

Driven by their desire to offer a unique healthcare experience that celebrates women, we have designed every element of our practice to ensure that you are left feeling cared for and valued.  We offer longer consultations, more comprehensive screenings and personalised medical support.
